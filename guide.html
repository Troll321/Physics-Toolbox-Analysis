- LSTsx, LSTsy, LSTsz, LSTWork, LSTDist represents the last data exist in the dataset

- Mass can be changed (assume its unit is kg)

- Formula Integral (velocity):
vel[t] = vel[t-1] + (dt*a[t-1])

- Formula Integral S (displacement):
s[t] = s[t-1] + dt*(vel[t-1]+vel[t])/2

- Formula Force:
f[t] = m*a[t]

- Formula Distance:
distance[t] = distance[t-1] + sqrt(|s[t]-s[t-1]|)
Assume disp is a vector and |VECTOR| is taking the magnitude

- Formula Work:
w[t] = w[t-1] + (f[t-1] . (disp[t]-disp[t-1]))
Assume F and disp is a vector and "." is a Dot Product operation between 2 vector

- The Total (ex: aTot, vTot, sTot) is calculated seperately by taking the magnitude of that vector
  Pytahogrean theorem for that: sqrt(ax^2 + ay^2 + az^2)

- Note: Distance is scalar while displacement is vector
